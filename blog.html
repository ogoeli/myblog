I AM BLACK,I AM ME!

January 21,2019
Posted by Precious

A very quick one (more like my rant on whatsapp),i just wanted to share my displeasure.My sister and I after dressing sweetly in our nike shoes we got as christmas gifts from our dad,went to a health facility today after school.We sat down waiting patiently to be called.Two asian looking women came in, one was aged and the other seemed like her daughter.The younger one went in to the counter while the older woman wanted to sit close to my sister ,then she looked up aaaaaand saw my sister and she moved to another sit near some mexican kids.Then she kept her face like agidi that soaked water.My sister and i were still trying to take that in then her daughter came and wanted to sit on that same sit .She looked up too and saw my sister then she moved away and stood by the door,when there were two chairs by my sisters right and left-hand side.

After a while,she came and sat down in a very funny way at the edge of the of the chair like she was wanted .My sister and i started laughing and we both said 'double wahala for dead body'.I kept looking at her and it made her more uncomfortable.They couldnt talk or even sneeze.

Image if my sister and i were not comfortable in our own skin or we had a problem with being black they would have would have won the battle.Hehehe they met the Ekwegh girls,fearless and confident to the brim.We no dey shake one bit and their case was no exceptional today.I wrote my final exams on trigometry today with a heart full of joy,i went to the place and they wanted to kill my joy,but God pass them,they met their matches.Be you,own your color and be proud of who you are.I am black nobody can make me uncomfortable because of my color.Dont let people that need melanin make you feel less of who you are.It is no sin to be black and if i was to choose.I would choose black always any day.Black is beautiful,be black,love black
